At Desteni we thoroughly research and investigate the essence of what it means to live responsibly and fully. This research is done through the participation of everyone that is interested and dares to take on the journey of self-discovery. We share our findings through video, audio and text through various internet media. This website gathers all the relevant information and links you'll want to explore if you're interested in learning about Desteni.
Enjoy!

the Desteni Material

EQAFE is our online shop. Here you can find tons of products covering just about every topic you can think of. For self-directed learning with relevant and up-to-date information, EQAFE is your resource of choice.

Desteni 'I' Process offers both courses and coaching. The Free DIP Lite course is for many the ideal way to become acquainted with the Desteni material and tools.

Self & Living is a series of videos with a focus on practical lifestyle creation, sharing simple yet effective solutions to common every-day-life issues and obstacles.

The S.O.U.L. material revolves around the role and power of words in our lives and provides the step-by-step support and know-how to live words as expressions of ourselves on our journey of self-creation.

The Destonian Wiki initiative was started to gather all the relevant posts from the original Desteni Forums and making them more easily available. The Destonian Wiki is currently offline for maintenance.

Hundreds of articles were written and released in the period from 2007-2010. When consulting the articles, hear the message/principles within them, yet always read them within the context of the time in which they were published – much has changed since.

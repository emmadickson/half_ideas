This moment is given to you, a gracious gift from God or the whole
or whatsoever you want to call it — Tao, dhamma, logos.
This moment is available to you: sing a song, live it in its totality.
And don´t try to sacrifice it for any other moment
that is going to come in the future.
Live it for its own sake.
